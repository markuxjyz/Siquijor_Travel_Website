Experience Siquijor

Famous tourist spots that astonish and mesmerize your heart, drawing you into the enchanting beauty and mystique of Siquijor. From breathtaking waterfalls and pristine beaches to historic landmarks and hidden caves, the island offers a perfect blend of adventure, relaxation, and cultural heritage. Every corner of this magical island promises an experience that will leave you in awe and longing to return.

Cambugahay Falls

Lazi, Siquijor

Dive into dreamy turquoise waters, swing from jungle vines, and let the cool cascades refresh your soul.

Salagdoong Beach

Maria, Siquijor

Feel the rush as you leap from towering cliffs into the crystal-clear sea, or simply soak up the sun on its powdery white sands.

Cantabon Cave

Cantabon, Siquijor

Channel your inner explorer as you navigate through this underground marvel adorned with mesmerizing stalactites, stalagmites, and shimmering pools.

Lazi Church and Convent

Lazi, Siquijor

Walk through history in this grand Spanish-era structure, a National Cultural Treasure that echoes centuries of faith and tradition.

San Juan Beach

San Juan, Siquijor

Stroll along golden shores, sip on fresh coconut juice, and witness some of the most breathtaking sunsets in the Philippines.

Mt. Bandilaan Nature Park

Siquijor, Siquijor

Conquer the island’s highest peak and be rewarded with panoramic views that stretch as far as the eye can see.

Old Enchanted Balete Tree

Lazi, Siquijor

Encounter Siquijor’s legendary 400-year-old tree and experience a one-of-a-kind natural fish spa as tiny fishes gently exfoliate your feet.

Guiwanon Spring Park

Siquijor, Siquijor

Wander along wooden bridges nestled among lush mangroves and embrace the serene, untouched beauty of nature.
